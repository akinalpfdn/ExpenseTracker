# Privacy Policy for Penko Expense Tracker

**Last Updated: January 2025**

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Penko Expense Tracker ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

## Data Collection and Storage

**Local Storage Only**: Penko Expense Tracker stores all your data locally on your device using CoreData/SQLite. We do NOT:
- Collect any personal information
- Upload your data to any servers
- Share your information with third parties
- Track your usage or behavior
- Access your contacts, location, or other device data

## Information We Store Locally

The following information is stored exclusively on your device:
- Expense records (amount, description, date, category)
- Categories and subcategories you create
- Budget limits (daily and monthly)
- Currency preferences
- Theme settings (dark/light mode)
- Recurring expense configurations
- Financial plans and projections

## Data You Control

Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- You have complete control over your data
- You can delete all data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in Settings
- Your data is NOT recoverable if you uninstall the app or lose your device
- We do NOT have access to your data
- We do NOT provide cloud backup or sync features

## Third-Party Services

### Apple In-App Purchase (StoreKit)
The app uses Apple's StoreKit framework for optional in-app donations/purchases. Apple's privacy policy applies to payment processing. We do NOT:
- Store payment information
- Access your payment details
- Track purchase history beyond what Apple provides

No other third-party services, analytics, or advertising networks are integrated into this app.

## Permissions

The app does not require any special permissions:
- **No location access**
- **No camera access**
- **No contacts access**
- **No photo library access**
- All data is stored in the app's secure sandbox

## Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. Since we don't collect any data at all, the app is safe for users of all ages and complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act).

## Data Security

Your data is protected by:
- iOS sandbox security
- Local storage only (no cloud transmission)
- Apple's built-in encryption for app data
- No third-party access

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
